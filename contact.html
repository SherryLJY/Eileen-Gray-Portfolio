Aram Designs


One of my last tasks in the early 1970s was to work with Zeev Aram on the introduction of my designs onto the world market. In 1973, I granted the worldwide rights to manufacture and distribute my designs to Aram Designs Ltd, London. Aram now holds the worldwide head license for my designs, and is the only UK source for the authentic products. Aram has granted a license to produce to ClassiCon of Germany.
